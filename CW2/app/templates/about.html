My Story:

I am a student at the University of Leeds. My origins are from the United Arab Emirates. I grew up in a house in the suburbs of Al Ain, where it's covered with golden dunes. I grew up with a lot of animals around me, and I always loved them. I wanted to help them but never knew how. I always thought that one day these animals will become extinct. So, I want to preserve them since they represent one of the national emblems in every country.

About Jungle:

Tucked away in the heart of the city, discover the Jungle Shop, a unique and colourful business dedicated to saving endangered animals. The moment you step inside, the rich, earthy scents of the wild blend with a kaleidoscope of hues. Each niche and shelf is adorned with an assortment of keepsakes and symbols, all of which tell a story about the amazing creatures that live in our planet's forests and jungles.

Our Mission

Our mission is to use community and technology to end animal extinction and environmental harm.

Our Vision

Our vision is to make the earth greener, more biodiverse, and able to support endangered species.
