Welcome to the Giraffe House

The Giraffe House is a non-profit organization that provides a safe haven for giraffes. We are located in the heart of the African Savannah, where giraffes are free to roam and live in their natural habitat. We have been in operation since 1999 and have helped over 100 giraffes find new homes. Our mission is to provide a safe haven for giraffes and educate the public about their plight. We are a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization and rely on donations to continue our work.
